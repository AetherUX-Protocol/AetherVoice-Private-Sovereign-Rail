# The AetherVoice "Selective Disclosure" Protocol

### Problem:
Traditional B2B trade requires privacy (to hide margins from competitors) but demands transparency (for tax and legal audits).

### Innovation:
AetherVoice utilizes **Umbra Viewing Keys** to break the binary choice between "Public" and "Secret." 

- **By Default:** The transaction is 100% confidential. No one can see the $1.5M fuel mandate amount.
- **On Demand:** The merchant can issue a temporary **Viewing Key** to an auditor.
- **The Evidence:** Under **BSA 2026**, the combination of the **QVAC local audit hash** and the **Umbra Decrypted Metadata** constitutes primary digital evidence of a legitimate business transaction.
